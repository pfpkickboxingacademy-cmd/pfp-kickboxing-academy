Open Film Study Sessions

Watch. Analyze. Improve.

Free every Wednesday for everyone

One of the best ways to grow as a martial artist is to study the game. Sensei Tyreeke Saint hosts regular Open Film Study Sessions on Discord, where members break down competition footage, analyze techniques, discuss strategy, and learn from high-level performances.

Together, we'll explore questions like:

  • Why did this technique score?
  • What adjustments could have been made?
  • How do elite competitors control distance and timing?
  • What can we apply to our own training?

Whether you're preparing for your next tournament or simply want to deepen your understanding of karate, these sessions are designed to help you see the sport from a new perspective.
