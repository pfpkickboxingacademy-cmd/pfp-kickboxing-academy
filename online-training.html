Train like a champion. Anywhere in the world.

Whether you're preparing for your next tournament or looking to sharpen your skills between competitions, Point Fighting University gives you access to elite-level coaching designed for serious martial artists.

Join the Online Program
Why Train Online?

Competition is won long before you step onto the mat.

This program was created for athletes who want more than another workout. It is designed for karate practitioners who are committed to improving their technique, refining their strategy, and developing the mindset required to compete at their highest level.

Train from anywhere while learning directly from Sensei Tyreeke Saint through structured lessons, detailed technical instruction, and competition-focused training.

Who It's For

Who this program is for.

This program is designed for:

  • Competitive karate athletes
  • Tournament competitors
  • Martial artists looking to improve kata and kumite
  • Students preparing for regional, national, or international competition
  • Karate practitioners who want coaching beyond their local dojo

If you're looking to become a better competitor, you're in the right place.
