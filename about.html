About PFP Kickboxing Academy

Building champions on and off the mat.

At PFP Kickboxing Academy, we believe martial arts is about more than learning techniques. It's about building confidence, discipline, resilience, and the mindset to overcome challenges in every area of life.

Our academy welcomes students of all ages and experience levels. Whether you're taking your very first class, looking for a new way to stay active, or pursuing excellence in competition, you'll find a supportive community committed to helping you reach your goals.

Every class is designed to challenge you physically, strengthen you mentally, and develop the character that extends far beyond the dojo.

Our Mission

Skill, confidence, and leadership.

Our mission is to empower every student through high-quality martial arts instruction that develops skill, confidence, and leadership.

We strive to create an environment where students feel encouraged to push their limits, celebrate their progress, and become the best version of themselves. Success isn't measured only by belts, medals, or trophies. It's measured by the confidence you gain, the discipline you build, and the person you become along the way.

Meet the Instructors

Taught by people who've won at the highest level.

Co-Founder and Head Instructor

Sensei Tyreeke Saint

Sensei Tyreeke Saint has dedicated his life to the study, practice, and teaching of martial arts. With years of experience as both a competitor and instructor, he is passionate about helping students unlock their full potential through technical excellence, discipline, and mentorship.

Whether he's introducing a beginner to their first stance or preparing an athlete for high-level competition, Sensei Tyreeke believes every student deserves the guidance and support to succeed.
