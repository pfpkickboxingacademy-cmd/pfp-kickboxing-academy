FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need experience to start?

Not at all! Our in-person classes are designed to welcome beginners while helping each student progress at their own pace.

What should I bring to my first class?

Wear comfortable athletic clothing and bring a water bottle. We'll guide you through everything else.

Do you offer classes for children and adults?

Yes! We offer programs for a variety of ages and skill levels.

Can I try a class before joining?

Absolutely. Your very first class is always free, just email or call us to get it scheduled. If you want more time to get a real feel for the academy, we also offer a discounted 2-week trial for $100, which you can sign up for here.
